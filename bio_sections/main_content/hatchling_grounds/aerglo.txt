Aerglo jist crawled oot ae the cauld, dark depths ae the sea wan day an intae the lair. Whit wis he daein doon there? Naebdy kens. Aw aebdy kens is: he's a pal an his hame's wae us noo. Th' ithir bairns love him an it wid be shan tae separate them, even if certain dragons (Pod) arenae shuir he's no some kind ae sea-demon.
